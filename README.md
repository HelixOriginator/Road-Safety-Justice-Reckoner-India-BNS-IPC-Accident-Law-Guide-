## ğŸš¨ What To Do Immediately After a Road Accident

1. Ensure your safety and move to a secure spot if possible
2. Call emergency numbers: 112 / local police / ambulance
3. Do NOT flee the scene (hit & run is a serious offence)
4. Take photos and videos of the scene
5. Note vehicle numbers, time and location
6. Help injured persons (Good Samaritan law protects you)
7. Give basic first aid if trained
8. Cooperate with authorities during FIR

## ğŸ¥ Hospital Admission Rules (Legal View)

- **No hospital can refuse to provide first aid or emergency care**
- Government & private hospitals must stabilize the victim
- Police formalities cannot delay treatment
- Identification is not mandatory before treatment
- Medical Examination Report (MLR) must be prepared
- Hospital must inform the police in medico-legal cases
- Patient rights include dignity, privacy & documentation

## ğŸ‘¨â€âš•ï¸ Duties & Responsibilities of Doctors

- Provide immediate emergency treatment
- Do not delay treatment due to paperwork or police
- Prepare a Medico Legal Case (MLC) report
- Inform nearest police station
- Maintain complete medical records
- If patient is unconscious: treat under implied consent
- If death occurs: preserve body and notify authorities

Doctors are legally protected while saving lives.

## âš–ï¸ If Death Occurs in an Accident

- Hospital must declare death & document time and cause
- Police conduct inquest under CrPC/BNS guidelines
- Post-mortem is conducted in suspicious/unnatural deaths
- Body is released to family after formalities
- Death certificate is issued by hospital/authority
- FIR is mandatory in fatal accident cases
- Family can file compensation claim (MACT / Insurance)
